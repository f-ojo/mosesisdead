About

Originally from Randallstown, MD - Baltimore-based singer/songwriter Mo is clearly influenced by every piece of music and art that crosses her path. Folk, R&B, grunge and jazz, a swirling tumult that gives her a uniquely CHARM CITY sound. With a voice careening from whispered intimacy to sultry, smoky power, she makes music that is angsty, soulful, raw - and she does it while supporting the communities around her: not just my own Baltimore-area open mics, but Lee Allen's Collaboration Laboratory, a powerful MIXER of musicians and artists based in Baltimore City. - Rob Hinkal: FocusMusic, Executive Director
